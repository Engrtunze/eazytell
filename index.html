What is EAZYtel and The Benefits?

Eazytel is a VoIP Telephony system that aids business communications and sales conversion.

Enhance customer service management

When customers make you a call, they expect that you handle them quickly, efficiently and in a professional manner.
This is where you can tap into the useful resources of call center softwares like EAZYtel.
EAZYtel software also enables the agents to access, collect and exchange information instantaneously in order
to provide a smooth transition among agents, thus dropping client wait times.

Improved Reporting Features

This amazing software provides business managers with all the necessary details they require for creating strategic plans and making important decisions.
Some of the reporting features that EAZYtel software assists the management with include

  • Upsell rates.
  • Revenue per call.
  • Case times.
  • Case categories.
  • Ability to evaluate call volume.

Enhance Productivity and Efficiency

EAZYtel software system helps boost overall productivity and enables telephone calls to be handled in an efficient manner.
When agents use the software, they are able to handle all inbound and outbound calls quickly and conveniently.
They are also able to track the calls easily and speed them up when needed.

Enhanced Data Access

With EAZYtel, agents have better access to a client’s data. Call center software organizes clients’ data centrally, having information about everything ranging from personal info to the clients’ purchase and service history. This allows agents to interact with clients in a more timely, comprehensive and customized manner.

Increased Communication and Responsibility

Since it is a centralized database, EAZYtel software allows employees across various departments to share and communicate correct information about clients through updates.
Moreover, call center software also offers greater accountability – every employee clearly understands personal responsibilities in sales processes and customer service so he or she can be held answerable for both success and failure.

Better Sales

EAZYtel Call center software has the power to drive sales instantly. This is indeed one of the greatest benefits of the software.
Using this software, sales managers can effectively handle the pre-sales queries of existing clients. They can easily have access to

  • Transaction history.
  • Process orders.
  • Check warranties.
  • Issue credits.
  • Handle after-sales services efficiently.

Great Customer Experience

Customer experience is improved when call center agents deliver appropriate and accurate information with a customized touch.
EAZYtel call center software streamlines data processing, thus it promotes increased customer retention and brand loyalty.
The database is also ideal for recording, observing and evaluating customer feedback about products and services.

Ease-to-use

Since businesses are migrating to cloud storage nowadays, you can seek call center solutions that are based on the virtual storage system that you can easily set up and start operating for your business.

Prioritizes clients and identify urgent calls

EAZYtel software comes with call prioritizing feature that allows you to identify calls that require immediate attention. Call prioritizing allows you to place your clients in sequential order based on the importance of their queries or requirements.

Multi-channel support

EAZYtel software helps you to connect with your clients via SMS, email, chat, voice calls, video, messaging apps, as well as social media platforms such as Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, etc. enabling your clients to pick the channel that is more convenient to them. Multi-channel support goes a long way in reducing customers’ frustrations, besides enabling you to offer enhanced customer service.
